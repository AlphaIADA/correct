Terms & Conditions

These terms summarize the primary trading disclosures, risk statements, and compliance expectations for SHARTFX clients.

Risk Warning

Trading leveraged forex and CFD products involves significant risk of loss and is not suitable for all investors. Market volatility, liquidity gaps, and platform outages may impact pricing, slippage, and execution of pending orders.

Profit expectation is based on clients risk appetite. At SHARTFX, Money and risk Management is our priority, and clients should trade only with capital they can afford to lose while monitoring exposure, margin requirements, and diversification.

Data & Privacy

Personal information supplied through web forms or Google Identity Services (GIS) is used solely to verify identity, offer platform access, and provide requested services. No passwords are stored until a secure backend is deployed; default authentication relies on Google Sign-In.

We collect metadata such as browser information, page path, and Google profile basics (ID, email, avatar) to help manage account requests. Data is processed per applicable privacy laws and retained only as long as necessary to satisfy regulatory requirements.

KYC / AML

Clients must supply accurate KYC documentation and keep profile details current. SHARTFX may suspend or terminate access if documents are invalid, expired, or inconsistent with regulatory directives. Withdrawals are processed only to verified beneficiaries that match the client profile, and additional verification may be requested before completion.

Complaints

Concerns about execution quality, platform access, social-trading performance, or affiliate conduct should be reported immediately via the support channels below. Provide account identifiers, ticket numbers, and a clear timeline so the compliance team can review logs and respond within a reasonable timeframe.
