Clients are required to supply accurate KYC information, keep login credentials secure, and immediately report unauthorized access. SHARTFX may suspend or terminate access if documentation is invalid, expired, or inconsistent with regulatory requirements.

Withdrawals are processed only to verified beneficiaries that match the client profile. Additional verification may be requested before a transfer is completed.

Access to the MT4/MT5, ECN PRO, SHARTFX PRO PLATFORM, and MAXIMUS PLATFORM environments is provided on an “as available” basis. Scheduled maintenance or unscheduled outages may occur, and SHARTFX is not liable for losses arising from latency, power failure, or third-party infrastructure issues.

Clients are encouraged to maintain redundant internet access and to contact SHARTFX support immediately when a platform disruption prevents them from managing positions.
